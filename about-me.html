About Me

Hello! My name is Tejas Waghmare, and I am a student from Pune, India. Currently, I am in the 11th standard, pursuing science with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM), and Information Technology.

I created Photo Enhancer because I had an idea for a tool that could make photo enhancement simple and accessible. While I know there are many similar tools available, I wanted to execute my own vision and learn through the process of building something from scratch.

As a student passionate about programming, I see every project as an opportunity to grow and apply my knowledge. This is just one of many projects I plan to develop as I continue learning new technologies and programming concepts.

Thank you for visiting my website! I hope you enjoy using Photo Enhancer as much as I enjoyed creating it.
