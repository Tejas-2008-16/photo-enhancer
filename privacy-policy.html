Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we may collect when you use our Photo Enhancer tool and how we safeguard your information.

Security: While we strive to keep your data secure, please ensure that you are using a safe network when accessing our website.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us.
